Creamy Ramen

This recipe details how to make a "Rich and Creamy Tonkotsu Ramen Broth", from the website "serious eats". I love making ramen, so I can't wait to try this out!

Ingredients

  • 3 Pounds of Pig Trotters
  • 2 Pounds Chicken Backs and Carcasses
  • 1 Large Onion
  • 12 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Knob Ginger
  • 2 Whole Leeks
  • 2 Dozen Scallions
  • 6 Ounces Whole Mushrooms
  • 1 Pound Slab Pork Fat Back
